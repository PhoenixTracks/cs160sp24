Forklift Safety

A forklift requires a keen awareness of safety protocols to prevent accidents and ensure a secure work environment. Before operation, it is crucial to perform a thorough pre-check of the forklift, including its tires, brakes, controls, warning devices, and mast. During operation, maintain a clear line of sight, adhere to load capacity limits, and navigate with caution, especially around pedestrians and in areas with high traffic. After operation, park the forklift in a designated area, lower the forks to the ground, and secure it from unauthorized use. Remember, safety is a shared responsibility; always wear your seatbelt, keep a vigilant eye, and follow the established safety guidelines to protect yourself and others.
